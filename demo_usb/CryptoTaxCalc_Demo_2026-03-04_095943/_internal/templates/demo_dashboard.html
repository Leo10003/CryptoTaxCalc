Workflow

Step 1 • Pick your data source: demo dataset or your own CSVs.

Load Demo Dataset

Reset and seed a clean demo DB.

FX rates (optional)

Import a CSV of daily EUR/USD rates to replace the demo 1.0 FX.

Expected columns: date and usd_per_eur (one row per day).

Daily prices for fee assets (optional)

If fees are paid in a third asset (e.g., BNB), import daily prices so fees can be valued correctly in EUR.

Template: prices_template.csv (date,base,quote,rate).

Upload CSVs

Multiple files supported (Binance, Coinbase, etc.).

Files stay on this machine. No trading data is sent to a server in this demo.

When you’re ready, continue to Stage 2 • Calculate to see a tax summary.

Step 2 • Run the calculation for this jurisdiction and review the preview.

Once the summary and charts look good, continue to Stage 3 • Export to generate reports.

Step 3 • Choose what should appear in your exported summary.

You can always go back to Stage 2 • Calculate to re-run a scenario; exports will always use your latest run.
